Paper Prototyping Round 1

Our first version of a paper prototype utilizes a scaled-up foam core model of an iPhone that users are instructed to treat as a regular iPhone, that is, features such as swiping and pinch-to-zoom are all available to them. This protoype has four main screens - really, three main screens where the second screen will display differently in portrait or landscape mode. It features autocomplete on searches, allows users to compare routes textually or graphically, and will quickly reroute a user if they miss their bus or train. It is currently undergoing user testing. Images of this version can be seen below.
