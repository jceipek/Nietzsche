The Problem

There are many “trip planner” applications available for smartphone users, but we feel that there is still tremendous room for improvement in the way users interact with the MBTA system through these applications. For example, many existing applications ignore variability of transit systems and give misleading departure/arrival times. Experienced users of the MBTA have an acquired body of knowledge that allows them to account for this inaccurate guidance, but many individuals who are new to or still learning the system rely on 3rd party assistance. We see an opportunity to create a mobile application that helps new individuals better explore the transit options available to them and make plans based on their unique needs and preferences.
